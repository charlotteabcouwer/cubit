Hi, and
welcome to
cubIT !

Want to get faster with your WCA events? Then this is the place to be! Scroll on quickly!

Every speed-cuber strives to become faster and faster every time, to achieve the lowest possible times with the cubes. This requires not only a lot of practice, but also the best algorithms!
On cubIT you can find the best algorithms from all WCA events, from PLL to multiple 3-style buffers. Practise your algorithms here and become faster and better at your favourite events!

The site is currently still under development and can only be used on laptops or computers. I hope that all speedcubers will be able to make optimal use of this site, (and maybe also app) in some time and will visibly improve their times with these algorithm trainers!
